The Problem

The internet is centralized. AI even more so.

⬡
Central AI APIs

All generative AI depends on centralized services controlled by a handful of corporations, creating single points of failure and control.

◈
Fragmented Education

Digital education is scattered across proprietary platforms with no interoperability, raising barriers and costs for users globally.

○
Isolated Games

Games are disconnected from open networks and decentralized identity, preventing a truly open gaming ecosystem.

Architecture

Four layers. One elastic grid.

LAYER 01
Protocol — Nostr
Events used as AI input/output. Decentralized identity. Censorship-resistant communication.
LAYER 02
Smart Relays
Relays running AI modules — specialized by domain: education, games, general assistance.
LAYER 03
Generative Models
Local or distributed models. Edge computing optimized. Low latency, high availability.
LAYER 04
Application
Nostr clients, games, educational platforms. Unified interface to the decentralized AI ecosystem.
Elasticity

Scales. Adapts. Resists.

⊞
Horizontal Scale

Multiple relays process requests simultaneously. No bottlenecks.

◎
Context Adaptation

Personalized responses for each user's context and Nostr identity.

⬡
Resilience

Failure of one node doesn't compromise the network. Truly distributed.
