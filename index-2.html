epNODE

Building 2026

epNODE is a comprehensive solution designed to simplify the setup and operation of a Bitcoin node integrated with Lightning Network and Nostr. The project addresses a critical gap in the Bitcoin ecosystem: the complexity barrier that prevents ordinary people from running their own nodes and participating in decentralized networks.

Built with user experience as the primary focus, epNODE streamlines the technical requirements while maintaining full security and privacy standards. It serves as both a practical tool and an educational platform, helping users understand the importance of node sovereignty while removing friction from the adoption process.

Core Components

Bitcoin Node (BTCPay)

Full blockchain validation and transaction verification for complete independence from centralized services

Lightning Network (LND)

Enable instant, low-cost payments and participate in the second-layer Bitcoin ecosystem

Nostr Integration

Decentralized identity, messaging, and social presence independent of platform control

Simplified UX

Intuitive interface that abstracts complexity without compromising security or transparency

Expected Impact

By lowering the barrier to entry for node operation, epNODE democratizes access to decentralized infrastructure. It empowers individuals to take control of their financial lives, protect their privacy, and participate in truly peer-to-peer economic systems without relying on intermediaries.

Bitcoin

The foundation of modern decentralized systems. Bitcoin represents a paradigm shift in how we think about money, value, and trust in networks. It's not just currency—it's a tool for freedom and individual sovereignty.

⚡

Lightning Network

Second-layer solution enabling instant, low-cost payments while maintaining Bitcoin's security guarantees. Lightning transforms Bitcoin from a settlement layer into a practical payments network for everyday transactions.

🌍

Nostr Protocol

Open protocol for decentralized, censorship-resistant social networks. Nostr enables individuals to own their identity and content, free from platform control or algorithmic manipulation.

Development Philosophy

We prioritize open-source development, allowing community scrutiny and contribution. Security is paramount—all code is designed to be auditable and transparent. User experience matters; we refuse to sacrifice usability for technical purity.

Every tool we build must respect user sovereignty, protect privacy, and enable people to maintain full control over their data and digital assets. These aren't optional features—they're foundational requirements.
